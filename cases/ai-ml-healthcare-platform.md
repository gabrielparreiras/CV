# Case: Cloud-Native AI/ML Platform for Healthcare Image Processing

## Context

During a critical healthcare context, a cloud-native platform was developed to support image processing, preliminary report generation and severity classification using AI/ML capabilities.

## Challenge

Reduce the time required to analyze medical imaging and generate preliminary reporting support, while enabling scalability, reliability and operational readiness.

Key challenges:

- Time-sensitive healthcare workflow.
- Need for cloud-native scalability.
- Integration of AI/ML capabilities with operational processes.
- Need to support availability and resilience for a high-impact use case.

## Approach

The solution connected cloud infrastructure, AI/ML processing, platform engineering and operational readiness.

Key practices:

- Cloud-native architecture to process imaging workloads.
- Machine Learning support for image classification and preliminary analysis.
- Operational integration to support processing flow and availability.
- Cross-functional collaboration across technology and business stakeholders.

## Business Impact

- Reduced preliminary medical report time from approximately 40 minutes to 1 minute.
- Demonstrated practical use of AI/ML in a business-critical and socially relevant context.
- Reinforced the value of cloud-native platforms for scalable and time-sensitive workloads.

## Leadership Lessons

- AI creates value when it is integrated into real workflows, not treated as an isolated experiment.
- Cloud-native architecture is a business enabler when latency, scalability and operational readiness matter.
- High-impact technology programs require strong connection between engineering, product, operations and stakeholder communication.
