# Framework: FinOps Maturity Model

## Purpose

Assess cloud financial management maturity across visibility, accountability, optimization and business value.

---

## Maturity Levels

| Level | Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Uncontrolled | Cloud spend is visible only after billing and ownership is unclear |
| 2 | Visible | Basic cost dashboards and budget tracking exist |
| 3 | Allocated | Costs are tagged, allocated and reviewed by owners |
| 4 | Optimized | Optimization routines are continuous and connected to engineering decisions |
| 5 | Value-Oriented | Cloud cost is managed through unit economics and business outcomes |

---

## Assessment Dimensions

| Dimension | Questions |
|---|---|
| Visibility | Do teams understand where cost comes from? |
| Ownership | Is every relevant cost assigned to an accountable owner? |
| Tagging | Is cost allocation reliable? |
| Forecasting | Are budget and consumption trends predictable? |
| Optimization | Are waste and rightsizing opportunities managed continuously? |
| Architecture | Is cost considered in design decisions? |
| Governance | Are policies and guardrails defined? |
| Value | Is cost connected to product or business metrics? |

---

## Recommended Outputs

- FinOps maturity score.
- Top cost drivers.
- Unallocated cost percentage.
- Waste reduction opportunities.
- Forecast accuracy.
- Tagging compliance.
- 90-day optimization roadmap.
